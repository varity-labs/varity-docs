## The problem

Traditional cloud platforms are powerful but expensive and complex:

- **Unpredictable pricing.** Hundreds of services, hidden costs, surprise bills.
- **Infrastructure overhead.** You manage load balancers, databases, CDNs, and scaling yourself.
- **DevOps tax.** Teams hire dedicated engineers just to keep infrastructure running.
- **Vendor lock-in.** Migrating away is slow and costly.

A typical app with 100 users, a database, storage, and AI features costs around **$2,800/month on AWS**.

## When to use Varity

- **Startups** -- Get to market fast without DevOps overhead.
- **Side projects** -- Ship without breaking the bank on infrastructure.
- **Enterprise apps** -- Save 70% on cloud costs while maintaining security.
- **Sensitive data** -- Healthcare, finance, or any regulated industry.

## When NOT to use Varity

- You need bare-metal access or extreme infrastructure customization.
- You already have a large AWS investment with reserved instances.
- You require specific compliance certifications we do not yet support (contact us for the roadmap).
- You need direct low-level control over servers and networking.

For most applications, Varity provides everything you need at a fraction of the cost.
